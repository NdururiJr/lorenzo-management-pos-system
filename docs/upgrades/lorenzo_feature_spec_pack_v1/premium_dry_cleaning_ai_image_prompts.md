# Premium Dry Cleaning Brand --- AI Image Generation Prompt System

Premium advertising prompts for AI image editors (ChatGPT/DALL-E,
Midjourney, Adobe Firefly) to transform dry cleaning, garment, and
facility photos into luxury, brand-ready marketing visuals.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Context

The brand aesthetic is clean, modern, luxurious, hygienic, and
trustworthy.

**Primary colors** - Deep navy (#0f172a) - Premium green (#16a34a) -
Soft blue (#0284c7)

**Supporting colors** - White (#ffffff) - Light gray (#F8FAFC) - Subtle
silver tones

**Emotional positioning** - Precision garment care - Professional
excellence - Trust and hygiene - Premium service quality

Visual reference level: Apple, Dyson, Mercedes-Benz, Ritz-Carlton.

# Base Prompt Template

Transform this dry cleaning or garment photo into a premium luxury
advertising image that conveys precision care, cleanliness, and
professional excellence. Maintain the subject identity exactly as shown
--- do not alter structure, garments, or components. Place the subject
in a pristine, minimal, luxury environment with soft white and light
gray gradients. Use professional studio lighting with soft key light,
fill light, and rim lighting. Enhance fabric textures to look crisp and
premium. Enhance metallic surfaces to look polished and refined. Apply
luxury commercial color grading with clean whites, soft shadows, and
natural highlights. Use shallow depth of field. Ultra-realistic,
cinematic commercial photography. Shot on Canon EOS R5, 85mm lens, f/4,
8K resolution.
