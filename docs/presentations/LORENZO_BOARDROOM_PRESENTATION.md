# Lorenzo Dry Cleaners Management System
## How The System Works - Boardroom Presentation

**Developer:** Jerry Ndururi in collaboration with AI Agents Plus
**Client:** Lorenzo Dry Cleaners, Nairobi, Kenya
**Status:** Production Ready (96%)

---

# What This System Does

A complete digital transformation of dry cleaning operations:

| Manual Process | Digital Solution |
|----------------|------------------|
| Paper receipts | Digital orders with tracking |
| Phone calls for updates | Automatic WhatsApp notifications |
| Manual route planning | AI-optimized delivery routes |
| Cash-only payments | M-Pesa, Card, and Credit |
| No customer visibility | Real-time tracking portal |
